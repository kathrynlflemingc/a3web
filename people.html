Who We Are

Kate

Kate has an MSc from Carleton University and thinks that geology is the schist. Her favourite mineral is Halite because she too is a bit salty. Kate spent 11 days in the desert, collecting 50 rocks for a 300 page thesis which would take her 2.5 years and one dangerous lab accident to complete. She is now pursuing a career in GIS to distance herself from deadly chemicals and to instead spend all of her time sitting behind a computer. Just like Carbon, Kate shines under pressure. When Kate grows up she hopes to become a GIS Applications Specialist.
