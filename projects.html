Spatial Analysis

Our spatial analysis division can conduct risk assessment and site suitability analyses for all of your environmental and municipal needs. Examples of our past projects include:

  • Watermain Break Risk Analysis
  • Flood Plain Analysis
  • Building Suitability Studies
  • Environmental Risk Analysis

Our team can sort through your topographic maps, aerial imagery, and tabular data to produce meaningful spatial analyses based on your specifications. Please contact us for more information on how we can help.

Geovisualization

Our cartography specialists can prepare a variety of reference or thematic maps for reporting, presentations, or displays. We can adapt our maps to suit digital or physical presentation media and are able to generalize your data to suit small format for printouts or pamphlets. Talk to us about your cartographic needs, and we will find ways to present your data clearly and attractively.

Database Modeling

Our team is skilled in database design, creating functional and efficient databases to geared towards your specific needs. Consult with us about your data storage requirements, and we will design a model that will both suit your current needs and plan for future ones as well. Our database services can be apploed to:

  • Spatial Datasets
  • Data Analytics
  • Human Resources

Remote Sensing

We have experience working with all manner of aerial and satellite imagery. Our expertise includes working with:

  • Spectral Index interpretation
  • Geocorrection and Orthorectification
  • Photogrammetry
  • Multispectral Image Classification (Supervised and Unsupervised)
  • Image Enhancement

Surveying

Our veteran surveying crew has years of experience under their belts and will brave the elements to carry out your surveying projects. Our dedicated AutoCAD department will create top quality survey plans from field data.

  • A team of hardened field surveyors who work effectively outdoors in any and all weather conditions
  • Conduct cadastral surveys
  • Creation of survey plans on AutoCAD from survey measurements

Our team is adept in working in AutoCAD to map your survey data clearly and accurately. If need be, our team is happy to truck the total stations and rods out to the field conduct cadastral surveys as well. Gets us out of the office!
