Spatial Analysis

Our spatial analysis division can conduct risk assessment and site suitability analyses for all of your environmental and municipal needs. Examples of our past projects include:

  • Watermain Break Risk Analysis
  • Flood Plain Analysis
  • Building Suitability Studies
  • Environmental Risk Analysis

Our team can sort through your topographic maps, aerial imagery, and tabular data to produce meaningful spatial analyses based on your specifications. Please contact us for more information on how we can help.

Geovisualization

Our cartography specialists can prepare a variety of reference or thematic maps for reporting, presentations, or displays. We can adapt our maps to suit digital or physical presentation media and are able to generalize your data to suit small format for printouts or pamphlets. Talk to us about your cartographic needs, and we will find ways to present your data clearly and attractively.
